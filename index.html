The Best Video Games of All Time

Yeah, here’s the twist: these aren’t the picks of a gamer — they’re the favorites of a cinephile

By

There are no beloved RPGs or strategy games here. There are also no one‑dimensional racing games, shooters, or fighting games with little to no decent storyline. This is just a list of my favorite games that have left a mark on my memory — a memory soaked in Hollywood culture. And yes, I’m not a hardcore gamer, and I haven’t played a lot of titles — but first, I hope to catch up someday and experience many legendary games, including Portal, Astro Bot, Mass Effect, and so on. Second, I have played some games after all, and I can make a choice based on that experience — so I’ve tried to explain each pick with solid reasoning.
